Design Rationale

The icon merges three identities: a radial gauge for real-time usage tracking, Claude's signature warm terracotta palette, and fiskaly's teal accent anchoring the fintech precision. The segmented arc reads as both a token meter and a progress dial. Three ascending bars inside reference analytics while forming a subtle rhythm. The dark ground gives it gravitas at small sizes; the luminous arcs ensure legibility in the menu bar.
